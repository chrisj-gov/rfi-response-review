1. Company Overview
Persistent Technology, Inc. (PTI) is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), ServiceNow Partner, and HUBZone firm headquartered in Alexandria, VA, specializing in humancentered IT modernization, enterprise case management, and platform sustainment for federal agencies. PTI is recognized for delivering transformative, human-centered solutions and providing leading-edge expertise in emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, natural language processing, extended reality, data modernization, and cyber-secure cloud solutions. The company has supported mission-critical platforms for the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), including the Standards & COTS Integration Platform (SCIP) and Supply Chain Management (SCM), and offers deep enterprise integration and modernization capabilities to help federal clients efficiently and securely achieve interoperability and modernization goals.
Core Capabilities for VA.gov Modernization
As veterans who actively use VA.gov, we recognize its significance both personally and professionally. Our mission centers on improving patient outcomes by leveraging our strengths in human-centered design, strategic communications, enterprise IT modernization, and interoperability. 4.3. Known Risks & Mitigation Strategies
Large federal digital modernization efforts, including VA.gov, face inherent risks such as integrating complex legacy systems, managing expanding volumes of content, addressing fragmented identity workflows, ensuring accessibility for all users, and operating within a highly visible and politically sensitive environment. If unmanaged, these risks can disrupt delivery and erode stakeholder and Veteran trust.
We mitigate these risks through a phased modernization approach with built-in validation checkpoints to reduce disruption and ensure compliance at each stage. Enterprise governance frameworks provide oversight and accountability, while automated accessibility testing prevents usability regression. Zerodowntime deployment practices preserve continuous access to services, and strict data governance safeguards sensitive information. Collectively, these measures reduce technical and operational risk while preserving a stable, trustworthy experience for Veterans and VA staff.
4.4. Lessons Learned from VA and Federal Platform Modernization
PTI's experience supporting VA and other federal platforms has consistently reinforced several key lessons:
